TEXAS DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION
REQUEST FOR QUALIFICATIONS — AERIAL SURVEY SERVICES

District: Houston
CSJ: 0912-72-225
Highway: IH-45 North Freeway Reconstruction (North Houston Highway Improvement Project)

SCOPE OF SERVICES:

TxDOT Houston District requires aerial LiDAR and photogrammetric survey
services for the IH-45 corridor from IH-610 North Loop to Beltway 8 North,
approximately 16 miles. This is a design-build project requiring current
topographic data for preliminary engineering.

SURVEY REQUIREMENTS:

A. AERIAL LIDAR ACQUISITION
   - USGS Quality Level 1 (QL1): 8 points/m² minimum
   - NVA ≤ 10.0 cm, VVA ≤ 15.0 cm
   - Full corridor width: 500 feet each side of centerline (1000 ft total)
   - Classified point cloud per ASPRS standards
   - Bare earth and above-ground returns

B. AERIAL PHOTOGRAPHY
   - 3-inch GSD orthomosaic minimum
   - True-color (RGB) and color-infrared (CIR) capture
   - Forward overlap 80%, sidelap 60%

C. GROUND CONTROL
   - GPS survey of ground control points at 1000-foot intervals
   - NAD83(2011) Texas State Plane, South Central Zone
   - NAVD88 vertical datum via GEOID18 model
   - Minimum 30 ground control panels

D. DELIVERABLES
   - LAS/LAZ point clouds (ASPRS Class 2, 6, 9 minimum)
   - 1-foot contour interval DTM/DSM
   - 3-inch GSD orthomosaic (GeoTIFF, MrSID)
   - Planimetric feature extraction (roads, buildings, drainage, utilities)
   - AutoCAD DGN and DWG files per TxDOT CAD Standards
   - Cross-sections at 100-foot stations
   - FGDC-compliant metadata

E. ACCURACY
   - Horizontal: ±0.5 foot (NSSDA 95%)
   - Vertical: ±0.3 foot hard surface, ±0.5 foot vegetated (NSSDA 95%)
   - 20 independent QC checkpoints required

ESTIMATED VALUE: $750,000 - $1,200,000
PERFORMANCE PERIOD: 120 calendar days from NTP
