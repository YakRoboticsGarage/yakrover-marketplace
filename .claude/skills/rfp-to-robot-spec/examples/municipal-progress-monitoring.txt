CITY OF ANN ARBOR — PURCHASING DEPARTMENT
REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL — CONSTRUCTION PROGRESS DOCUMENTATION

Project: Stadium Boulevard Reconstruction, Phase 2
RFP No: AA-PW-2026-018

SCOPE:

The City of Ann Arbor seeks proposals for monthly aerial progress
documentation of the Stadium Boulevard reconstruction project between
South Main Street and South State Street (approximately 1.2 miles).
The contractor will provide recurring monthly drone surveys over a
14-month construction period.

REQUIREMENTS:

1. MONTHLY AERIAL SURVEY (14 visits)
   - Orthomosaic photography at ≤2-inch GSD
   - LiDAR point cloud for volumetric earthwork comparison
   - Comparison overlay with design surface to quantify cut/fill progress
   - Weather-dependent scheduling with 3-day makeup window

2. VOLUMETRIC ANALYSIS
   - Earthwork quantity comparison: actual vs. plan
   - Monthly cut/fill volume report in cubic yards
   - Stockpile measurement at contractor staging areas
   - Accuracy: ±3% volumetric at 95% confidence

3. PHOTOGRAPHIC DOCUMENTATION
   - Minimum 200 oblique photos per visit covering full corridor
   - Timestamped and geotagged
   - Before/after comparison views at 10 key locations
   - Web-accessible photo gallery for stakeholder review

4. DELIVERABLES PER VISIT
   - GeoTIFF orthomosaic
   - LAS point cloud
   - Volumetric report (PDF + CSV)
   - Photo gallery (web link)
   - Progress comparison map (design overlay)

5. COORDINATE SYSTEM
   - Michigan State Plane South Zone
   - NAD83(2011), NAVD88
   - Tie to City of Ann Arbor survey monuments

QUALIFICATIONS:
   - FAA Part 107 certified pilot
   - 3+ years construction progress monitoring experience
   - References from 2 municipal or DOT clients
   - Insurance: $2M general liability, $1M professional

BUDGET: Not to exceed $5,600 per visit ($78,400 total for 14 visits)
PERIOD: April 2026 through May 2027
