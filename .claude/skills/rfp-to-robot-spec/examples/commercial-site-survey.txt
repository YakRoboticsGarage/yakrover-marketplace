I need a full pre-bid survey package for the Kroger distribution center
expansion in Romulus. The site is approximately 45 acres — existing
facility plus the proposed expansion area to the south. We're bidding
the site grading, utilities, and paving package.

WHAT I NEED:

1. Topographic survey of the full 45 acres including:

   - Overhead and underground electric

4. Environmental baseline:
   - Identify wetland boundaries (if any) for EGLE permitting
   - Note existing drainage outfalls

DELIVERABLES:

2 weeks of authorization.
