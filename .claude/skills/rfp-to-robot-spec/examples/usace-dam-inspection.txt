U.S. ARMY CORPS OF ENGINEERS — DETROIT DISTRICT
SOLICITATION — DAM SAFETY INSPECTION AND MONITORING SURVEY

Solicitation No: W911XK-26-R-0047
Project: Periodic Inspection — Coralville Dam, Iowa City, IA

SCOPE:

The USACE Detroit District requires surveying and inspection services for
the Coralville Dam as part of the Dam Safety Program per ER 1110-2-1156.
Work includes deformation monitoring survey, condition inspection, and
underwater inspection of outlet works.

SURVEY REQUIREMENTS:

1. DEFORMATION MONITORING
   - Establish monitoring network: minimum 12 monuments on dam crest
   - Precision: ±0.01 foot horizontal, ±0.005 foot vertical
   - Baseline survey plus 4 quarterly monitoring epochs
   - Settlement plates at 6 locations on embankment
   - Compare results to historical deformation data (2018-2025)

2. TOPOGRAPHIC SURVEY OF DAM AND SPILLWAY
   - 1-foot contour interval for entire dam footprint (~40 acres)
   - Include spillway, stilling basin, outlet works, and abutments
   - Hard surface elevations to ±0.02 foot
   - Coordinate system: Iowa State Plane South, NAD83(2011), NAVD88

3. 3D LASER SCANNING
   - Terrestrial LiDAR scan of spillway and outlet portal
   - Point spacing ≤ 0.05 foot on concrete surfaces
   - Color point cloud (RGB)
   - Registration accuracy: ≤ 0.02 foot RMS
   - Deliverable: e57 and LAS format

4. UNDERWATER INSPECTION
   - Dive inspection of outlet conduit (8-foot diameter, 800 linear feet)
   - Video documentation with distance markers
   - Measure concrete deterioration, joint condition, sediment depth
   - Ultrasonic thickness gauging of steel liner (if present)

5. UAS INSPECTION
   - Drone photography of upstream and downstream faces
   - Thermal imaging for seepage detection
   - Minimum 1-inch GSD for condition documentation
   - FAA Part 107 and COA for restricted airspace near dam

DELIVERABLES:
   - Deformation analysis report with movement vectors
   - Topographic surface model (LandXML, DXF)
   - 3D point clouds (e57, LAS)
   - Underwater inspection video and report
   - UAS photo documentation and thermal analysis
   - Periodic Inspection Report per USACE format

PERSONNEL REQUIREMENTS:
   - Licensed Professional Surveyor
   - Registered Professional Engineer (dam safety experience)
   - ADCI-certified commercial divers
   - FAA Part 107 + COA for restricted airspace
   - USACE CQM certification for QC manager

PERIOD OF PERFORMANCE: March 2026 - March 2027 (baseline + 4 quarterly epochs)
ESTIMATED VALUE: $280,000 - $350,000
