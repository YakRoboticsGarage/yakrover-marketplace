FEDERAL AVIATION ADMINISTRATION
TASK ORDER — AERONAUTICAL SURVEY

Airport: Gerald R. Ford International Airport (GRR), Grand Rapids, MI
Contract: DTFAWA-16-D-00008, Task Order 0042

SCOPE:

FAA requires an aeronautical survey update for GRR to support the Runway
8R/26L extension project. Survey must comply with AC 150/5300-18B.

SURVEY REQUIREMENTS:

1. RUNWAY SURVEY
   - Full runway profile: Runway 8R/26L (10,000 ft x 150 ft)
   - Pavement elevations at 10-foot grid
   - Accuracy: ±0.05 ft horizontal, ±0.01 ft vertical on pavement
   - Edge lights, PAPI, and MALSR positions

2. OBSTRUCTION SURVEY
   - All objects penetrating FAR Part 77 imaginary surfaces
   - 10,000-foot radius from ARP
   - Accuracy: ±1.0 ft horizontal, ±0.5 ft vertical
   - Tree heights, buildings, towers, terrain high points

3. APPROACH SURVEY
   - ILS critical areas for Runway 26L
   - Terrain along approach path: 5-mile extended centerline
   - 1-foot contour interval within approach surface

4. DELIVERABLES
   - FAA Survey Database (ASCII, NGS format)
   - AutoCAD DWG per FAA CAD standards
   - 6-inch GSD orthomosaic of airfield
   - Obstruction Analysis/FAR Part 77 surface penetration report
   - NAVD88 vertical datum, NAD83 State Plane Michigan South

RESTRICTIONS:
   - All airfield operations require airfield driver training
   - Drone ops require FAA COA (no Part 107 waivers on certificated airports)
   - Night operations only on active runways
   - Escort required at all times in AOA

ESTIMATED VALUE: $180,000 - $220,000
PERIOD: 90 calendar days
