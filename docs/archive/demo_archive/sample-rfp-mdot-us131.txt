MICHIGAN DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION
REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL — SURVEY SERVICES

Project: US-131 Resurfacing, Kalamazoo County
Control Section: 39042
Job Number: 208432

SCOPE OF WORK:
The Michigan Department of Transportation requires survey services for the
proposed resurfacing of US-131 from mile marker 36.2 to 42.8 in Kalamazoo
County. The project area encompasses approximately 6.6 miles of divided highway.

SURVEY REQUIREMENTS:

1. TOPOGRAPHIC SURVEY
   - Full topographic survey of existing roadway and 50-foot offset each side
   - Cross-sections at 50-foot intervals per MDOT Section 104.09
   - Hard surface elevations to nearest 0.01 foot
   - Ground elevations to nearest 0.1 foot
   - All drainage structures, utilities, signs, and appurtenances

2. CONTROL SURVEY
   - Primary control points at maximum 1000-foot intervals
   - Bench marks every 1000 feet
   - Michigan State Plane Coordinate System, South Zone
   - NAD83(2011) horizontal datum, NAVD88 vertical datum

3. PAVEMENT CONDITION DATA
   - Rutting depth measurements at 50-foot intervals
   - Joint/crack mapping
   - Pavement thickness verification (minimum 5 cores per mile)

4. DELIVERABLES
   - LandXML surface model
   - AutoCAD DXF with layers per MDOT standards
   - Cross-section CSV files at 50-foot stations
   - GeoTIFF orthomosaic at 2-inch GSD minimum
   - LAS point cloud (ASPRS Class 2 ground)
   - Survey report with NSSDA accuracy statement

5. ACCURACY REQUIREMENTS
   - Horizontal: ±0.05 feet (95% confidence)
   - Vertical: ±0.05 feet on hard surfaces
   - Point cloud validation within 0.05 feet per MDOT Chapter 9
   - NCHRP Report 748 Category 1A compliance for mobile LiDAR

6. CERTIFICATIONS
   - Michigan Licensed Professional Surveyor required
   - FAA Part 107 for aerial operations
   - OSHA 10-hour for field crew
   - MOT (Maintenance of Traffic) certified

TIMELINE:
   - Survey must be completed by April 30, 2026
   - Letting date: August 2026

BUDGET: Not to exceed $45,000 for all survey services.

Submit proposals to MDOT Region Office, Kalamazoo.
